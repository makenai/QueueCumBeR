About

This is quick for-fun project created to get @makenai familiar with Google App Engine again. It was sort of inspired by @zachware who was complaining that there was nothing good to watch on Netflix Instant. I wanted to share my instant queue and couldn't find a quick and easy way to do it, so I hacked together a PHP script. This is the evolution of that PHP script.

Incidentally, this is my personal instant queue. I don't remove things from there, instead it's just a collection of movies I'm likely to watch now and then while doing something else.
